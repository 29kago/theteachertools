Free Classroom Tools, Built for Real Teachers

The Teacher Tools is a free collection of simple, projector-friendly classroom apps that run right in your web browser. There is no sign-up, no download and no software to install — just open a tool and start teaching. Every tool is designed to be big, clear and easy to read from the back of the room, so it works beautifully on an interactive whiteboard, a projector, a laptop or a tablet.

We built these tools to take small, repetitive classroom jobs off your plate — calling on students fairly, arranging a seating chart, tracking behaviour, rewarding good choices and timing activities — so you can spend less time managing and more time teaching. They are free for every educator, anywhere in the world, and always will be.

Our classroom tools

Random Student Picker Wheel

Spin a name wheel to call on students fairly and keep the whole class engaged. Paste your class list, spin, and remove names already chosen so nobody is picked twice.

Classroom Seating Chart Maker

Drag desks into place to match your real room, then assign or shuffle students into seats in minutes. Save a layout for each class and reuse it all year.

Classroom Behavior Chart

A colour-coded clip chart for tracking conduct through the day. Students clip up for positive choices and down when they need a reminder, with every level visible on the board.

Good Noodles Reward Chart

A playful positive-reward board that celebrates kindness, effort and focus. Hand out a good noodle when you catch a student doing the right thing and watch it spread.

Classroom Timer

A big, high-contrast countdown the whole class can read from across the room. Perfect for timed tests, transitions, brain breaks and clean-up, with a clear alarm when time is up.

Why teachers choose The Teacher Tools

  • Free forever. Every tool is completely free, with no trial, no paywall and no premium tier.
  • No sign-up, no download. Open a tool and start using it straight away — there is nothing to install or set up.
  • Made for the big screen. Large, high-contrast designs are easy to read on projectors and interactive whiteboards.
  • Privacy-friendly. Class lists are saved on your own device, not on our servers, so your students' names stay private to you.
  • Works on any device. The tools run in any modern browser on a laptop, tablet, phone or smartboard.

How it works

  1. Pick a tool from the tabs above or the list of tools on this page.
  2. Add your class — type or paste your students' names once, or load a class you have saved.
  3. Put it on the big screen and teach. Spin the wheel, start the timer, clip a student up, or hand out a good noodle.

Frequently asked questions

Are The Teacher Tools really free?

Yes. Every tool is completely free to use, with no trial period and no premium upgrade. If you would like to help cover our hosting costs, you can support us on Ko-fi, but it is never required.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is needed. There is no log-in and no email to enter — just open a tool and use it. You can optionally save your classes on your own device for next time.

Is my students' data safe?

Yes. The names and class lists you enter are stored locally in your own browser, not sent to or stored on our servers, so they stay private to you.

Do the tools work on a smartboard or tablet?

Absolutely. Every tool is touch-friendly and scales to fit interactive whiteboards, projectors, laptops, tablets and phones, so it fits whatever device runs your classroom.

Which tool should I start with?

Most teachers start with the Random Student Picker Wheel for fair participation or the Classroom Timer for pacing activities. From there, explore the Seating Chart Maker, Behavior Chart and Good Noodles reward board.
