Contact Us

We would love to hear from you. Whether you have found a bug, want to suggest a feature, have a question about one of the tools, or just want to say hello, send us a message and we will get back to you.

Email

The best way to reach us is by email at kaganaydogan87@gmail.com. We aim to reply within a few business days.

What to get in touch about

  • Feedback and ideas. Many of our features come straight from teacher suggestions. If a tool would help your classroom, tell us.
  • Bug reports. If something is not working as expected, let us know the tool, your device and browser, and what happened, and we will look into it.
  • Questions. Not sure how a tool works or whether it can do what you need? Ask away.
  • Content and corrections. If you spot an error in one of our teaching articles, we would be grateful to hear about it.

Looking for something specific?

  • Read how we handle your data on our Privacy Policy page.
  • Learn more about the project on our About page.
  • Browse all of our free classroom tools and teaching resources.

Support the project

The Teacher Tools is free and always will be. If our tools save you time and you would like to help cover hosting costs, you can support us on Ko-fi. It is never required, and it genuinely helps keep the lights on.
